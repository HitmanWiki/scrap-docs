Channel Monitoring

GHOSTwire monitors Telegram channels 24/7 for token signals. When a contract address is detected, it automatically executes a buy using your configured settings.

How It Works

Add any Telegram channel → Bot monitors for Solana addresses → Auto-snipes when a signal appears → Sends you buy confirmation → Auto-sells at your take profit %

Step 1: Choose Channel Type

Click Add Channel from the main menu to start.

🌐
Public Channels
Works for any public Telegram channel. No authentication needed.
🔒
Private Channels
Requires TG Auth. You connect your Telegram account via QR or OTP.

Auto-Snipe in Action

When a message with a contract address appears in a monitored channel:

The bot:

  • Extracts the Solana address
  • Checks if you already hold it (48h cooldown)
  • Verifies sufficient SOL balance
  • Executes buy via Jupiter API
  • Sends confirmation with TX link
  • Updates your portfolio

Channel FAQ

How many channels can I add?

No hard limit. Add as many as you want!

Why isn't my private channel working?

You need to complete TG Auth first. Go to Main Menu → TG Auth → Connect Session (QR or OTP).

Can I change settings after adding?

Yes. Remove the channel and re-add with new settings, or configure per-wallet defaults in Wallet Settings.

What happens if the bot is offline?

GHOSTwire runs 24/7. If offline for maintenance, missed signals won't be processed.
