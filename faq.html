❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about GHOSTwire. Click on any question to expand the answer.

🚀 Getting Started

How do I start using GHOSTwire?▶
Simply open @ghowr_bot on Telegram and send /start. Your W1 wallet is created automatically. Fund it with SOL and start trading!
Is my private key stored anywhere?▶
No. GHOSTwire uses derived wallets — your private key is mathematically derived from your Telegram ID + a secret. It's never stored in any database.
Do I need to create a wallet manually?▶
No. When you first run /start, GHOSTwire automatically creates your W1 wallet. You can create up to 5 wallets from the Wallets menu.

📈 Trading

How do I buy a token?▶
Click 📈 Buy Token, then send the token address or DexScreener URL. Review and confirm. The bot executes via Jupiter API.
How do I sell my tokens?▶
Click 📉 Sell Token, select the token, choose percentage (100%, 50%, 25%, 10%) or custom amount. Confirm to sell.
What is auto-snipe?▶
Auto-snipe automatically buys tokens when contract addresses are detected in your monitored Telegram channels.
What is auto-sell?▶
Auto-sell automatically sells your position when profit reaches your target %. Set Take Profit in Settings.
Why didn't my buy go through?▶
Common reasons: insufficient SOL, daily limit reached, 48h cooldown on held tokens, or slippage too low.

💼 Wallets

How many wallets can I have?▶
Up to 5 wallets (W1-W5). Each has independent settings for buy amount, slippage, and take profit.
How do I export my private key?▶
Go to 💼 Wallets → Select wallet → 🔑 Export Private Key. Never share this key!
Can I import my existing Solana wallet?▶
Yes. Use 🔒 Add Private from the wallet menu to import an existing private key.

🔒 Private Channels

How do I monitor private channels?▶
Complete 🔐 TG Auth first. Then add channel via ➕ Add Channel → 🔒 Private with channel ID.
How do I get a private channel ID?▶
Forward a message from the channel to @userinfobot. It replies with the ID.
Do I need to disable 2FA?▶
Temporarily disable for easiest setup, then re-enable. Or connect with 2FA by entering your password.

💰 Fees & Referrals

What is the trading fee?▶
0.5% per trade (buy and sell). Shown in every transaction notification.
How do I earn from referrals?▶
Share your referral link from 🔗 Referral menu. Earn 20% of their trading fees forever.
How do I withdraw referral earnings?▶
Contact the bot admin to withdraw accumulated referral commissions.

🔧 Technical

Is GHOSTwire safe?▶
Yes. Derived wallets, no private key storage, trades via Jupiter API. Use a dedicated trading wallet.
What if the bot goes offline?▶
Missed signals won't process. Your funds remain safe in your wallet. Bot runs 24/7.
Can I use on multiple devices?▶
Yes. GHOSTwire is a Telegram bot — access from any device with Telegram.
How do I get support?▶
Contact the bot admin via Telegram. Include screenshots and error messages if possible.
