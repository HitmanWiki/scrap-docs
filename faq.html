❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about GHOSTwire. Click on any question to expand the answer.

🚀 Getting Started

How do I start using GHOSTwire? ▶
Simply open @GHOSTwirescrap_bot on Telegram and send /start. Your W1 wallet is created automatically. Fund it with SOL and start trading!
Is my private key stored anywhere? ▶
No. GHOSTwire uses derived wallets — your private key is mathematically derived from your Telegram ID + a secret. It's never stored in any database. Only you can access it via the Export feature.
Do I need to create a wallet manually? ▶
No. When you first run /start, GHOSTwire automatically creates your W1 wallet. You can create up to 5 wallets (W1-W5) from the Wallets menu.

📈 Trading

How do I buy a token? ▶
Click 📈 Buy Token, then send the token address or DexScreener URL. Review the confirmation and click Confirm. The bot executes the swap via Jupiter API.
How do I sell my tokens? ▶
Click 📉 Sell Token, select the token, choose percentage (100%, 50%, 25%, 10%) or enter custom amount. Confirm to sell.
What is auto-snipe? ▶
Auto-snipe automatically buys tokens when contract addresses are detected in your monitored Telegram channels. Add channels via ➕ Add Channel and enable Auto-Buy in Settings.
What is auto-sell? ▶
Auto-sell automatically sells your position when the profit percentage reaches your target. Set Take Profit % in Settings and enable Auto-Sell.
Why didn't my buy go through? ▶
Common reasons: insufficient SOL balance, daily limit reached, already holding the token (48h cooldown), or slippage too low. Check your balance and settings.

💼 Wallets

How many wallets can I have? ▶
You can have up to 5 wallets (W1, W2, W3, W4, W5). Each wallet has independent settings for buy amount, slippage, and take profit.
How do I export my private key? ▶
Go to 💼 Wallets → Select a wallet → Click 🔑 Export Private Key. Never share this key with anyone!
Can I import my existing Solana wallet? ▶
Yes. Use 🔒 Add Private from the wallet menu to import an existing private key. The bot will derive the public address automatically.

🔒 Private Channels

How do I monitor private channels? ▶
First complete 🔐 TG Auth (QR or OTP login). Then add channel via ➕ Add Channel → 🔒 Private and enter the channel ID (format: -100XXXXXXXXXXXXX).
How do I get a private channel ID? ▶
Forward a message from the private channel to @userinfobot. It will reply with the channel ID in format -100XXXXXXXXXXXXX.
Do I need to disable 2FA to connect? ▶
For easiest setup, temporarily disable 2FA before connecting, then re-enable after. The bot will keep working. You can also connect with 2FA enabled by entering your password when prompted.

💰 Fees & Referrals

What is the trading fee? ▶
GHOSTwire charges 0.05% per trade (buy and sell). The fee is automatically deducted and shown in every transaction notification.
How do I earn from referrals? ▶
Share your unique referral link from 🔗 Referral menu. When someone joins using your link, you earn 20% of their trading fees forever.
How do I withdraw my referral earnings? ▶
Contact the bot admin to withdraw your accumulated referral commissions. Earnings are tracked in your referral stats.

🔧 Technical

Is GHOSTwire safe to use? ▶
Yes. GHOSTwire uses derived wallets (no private key storage), reads only channel messages (cannot send as you), and executes trades via Jupiter API. Use a dedicated trading wallet for best security.
What happens if the bot goes offline? ▶
GHOSTwire runs 24/7. If offline for maintenance, missed signals won't be processed. Your funds remain safe in your wallet.
Can I use GHOSTwire on multiple devices? ▶
Yes. GHOSTwire is a Telegram bot — access it from any device with Telegram installed (phone, desktop, web).
How do I report a bug or get support? ▶
Contact the bot admin via Telegram or join the official support group. Include screenshots and error messages if possible.
