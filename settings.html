Troubleshooting

Settings not saving?

Settings save automatically. Try /start to refresh or contact support.

Auto-sell not triggering?

Check: Auto-Sell is ON, Take Profit % > 0, Position is in profit at target %.

Auto-buy not working?

Check: Auto-Buy is ON, Channels added, Daily limit not reached, Sufficient SOL.

Settings FAQ

Do settings apply to all wallets?

No! Each wallet has independent settings.

Different settings per channel?

Yes! Override settings when adding a channel.

Maximum slippage?

No hard limit, but 20-25% is practical maximum.

Set take profit to 0?

Yes. Disables auto-sell for that wallet/channel.
