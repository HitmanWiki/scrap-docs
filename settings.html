Settings

Configure GHOSTwire to match your trading style. All settings are saved per user and per wallet.

Settings Overview

• Default Buy Amount — SOL amount per trade
• Default Slippage — % tolerance for price movement
• Take Profit % — Auto-sell when profit hits this %
• Auto-Sell — Enable/disable automatic selling
• Auto-Buy — Enable/disable auto-sniping from channels

Troubleshooting

Settings not saving?

Settings save automatically. If not updating, try /start to refresh or contact support.

Auto-sell not triggering?

Check: 1) Auto-Sell is ON, 2) Take Profit % > 0, 3) Position is in profit at target %.

Auto-buy not working?

Check: 1) Auto-Buy is ON, 2) Channels are added, 3) Daily limit not reached, 4) Sufficient SOL balance.

Settings FAQ

Do settings apply to all wallets?

No! Each wallet has independent settings. Configure per wallet via Wallets → Select Wallet → Settings.

Can I have different settings per channel?

Yes! When adding a channel, you can override buy amount, slippage, and take profit for that specific channel.

What's the maximum slippage allowed?

No hard limit, but 20-25% is the practical maximum for most tokens. Higher than that may cause failed transactions.

Can I set take profit to 0?

Yes. Take profit = 0 disables auto-sell for that wallet/channel.
