Referral Rewards & Token Holdings

Open Referral System

Any user can generate and share a referral link. Referrers earn 20% of fees generated by referred users. No $GHOST holding requirement exists to participate.

Holder-Gated Claims (Planned)

The referral system will remain open to everyone, however claiming rewards will require verified $GHOST holdings.

  • Anyone can continue referring new users
  • Referral rewards will continue accumulating regardless of token holdings
  • To withdraw or claim accumulated rewards, users must hold the required amount of $GHOST
  • $GHOST holders receive priority access to referral reward claims and future referral program enhancements
Ecosystem Participation

This model encourages ecosystem participation while creating additional utility and demand for the $GHOST token.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need $GHOST to use the referral program?

No! Anyone can generate and share a referral link and earn 20% of referral fees. No token holding required.

When will I need $GHOST for referrals?

When the holder-gated claims feature launches, you'll need to hold $GHOST to withdraw or claim accumulated referral rewards. Referral earnings will continue accumulating regardless.

What exclusive access do holders get?

$GHOST holders will gain access to token-gated private communities, premium trading groups, exclusive discussions, and specialized scalping channels.

What premium features are planned?

As GHOSTwire expands, new holder-only tools, automations, analytics, and trading features will be introduced exclusively for $GHOST holders.

Growing Ecosystem

The utility of $GHOST is designed to grow alongside the platform. As new products, integrations, and services are released, additional benefits and use cases may be added for token holders.
