Referral FAQ

How do I get my referral link?

Click Referral from main menu → Your link is displayed.

How much commission do I earn?

20% of all trading fees paid by your referrals. Base fee is 0.1%, so you earn 0.02% of their trade volume.

How long do I earn commissions?

Forever! No expiration. You earn on all their future trades.

Can I refer myself?

No. System prevents self-referrals.

How are commissions paid?

Accumulated automatically. Contact admin for withdrawal.

What if my friend already used the bot?

Referral only works for new users. Existing users cannot be retroactively referred.
