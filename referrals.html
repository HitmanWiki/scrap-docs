Referral FAQ

How do I get my referral link?

Click Referral from main menu → Your link is displayed. Click "Copy Link" to share.

How much commission do I earn?

20% of all trading fees paid by your referrals. The base fee is 0.05%, so you earn 0.01% of their trade volume.

How long do I earn commissions?

Forever! Once someone is your referral, you earn commissions on all their future trades. No expiration.

Can I refer myself?

No. The system prevents self-referrals. You cannot earn commissions from your own trades.

How are commissions paid?

Commissions are tracked in your referral stats. They accumulate automatically. Contact admin for withdrawal.

What if my friend already used the bot?

Referral only works for new users clicking your link for the first time. Existing users cannot be retroactively referred.
