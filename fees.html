Fee System

GHOSTwire charges a small fee on each trade to cover development, infrastructure, and server costs. Fees are automatically deducted and clearly shown in every transaction notification.

Fee Overview

• Base Fee: 0.05% per trade
• Referral Commission: 20% of fee goes to your referrer (if you joined via referral link)
• Whitelist: Some users may be whitelisted and pay 0% fees
• Transparency: Fee amount is always shown in buy/sell notifications

How Fee is Calculated

Trade Amount: 1.0 SOL
Fee (0.05%): 0.0005 SOL
├── Referrer (20%): 0.0001 SOL
└── Operations (80%): 0.0004 SOL
You receive: 0.9995 SOL
Example: 10 SOL Trade
Trade: 10 SOL
Fee (0.05%): 0.005 SOL
→ Referrer: 0.001 SOL
→ Operations: 0.004 SOL
You receive: 9.995 SOL

Referral Commission

If you joined GHOSTwire via someone's referral link, 20% of the fees you pay go to your referrer. This doesn't cost you extra — it's just a portion of the standard fee.

Important

You pay the same 0.05% fee regardless of whether you have a referrer. The referrer's commission comes from the fee, not from your pocket.

Where Fees Go

🏢
80% - Operations
Server costs, RPC endpoints, development, maintenance, and support
🔗
20% - Referrals
Distributed to users who referred you (if applicable)

Fee FAQ

Why is there a fee?

Fees cover server costs, RPC expenses, development, and maintenance of GHOSTwire. They also fund the referral program.

How can I check the fee on my trade?

Every buy and sell notification includes the fee amount and percentage. Look for "💰 Fee: X SOL (0.05%)" in the message.

Are fees charged on sells too?

Yes, fees apply to both buy and sell transactions. The fee is calculated on the SOL amount of the trade.

What does "Whitelisted" mean?

If you see "💰 Fee: 0.000000 SOL (Whitelisted)" in your notifications, you're on the whitelist and pay 0% fees.

How do I get whitelisted?

Whitelist is managed by the bot admin. It's typically for early supporters, testers, or special cases.

Does my referrer get paid from my fees?

Yes, if you joined via a referral link, your referrer earns 20% of the fees you pay. This doesn't increase your fee — it's just a portion of the standard 0.05%.

Summary
  • Fee: 0.05% per trade (buy and sell)
  • Always shown in transaction notifications
  • Whitelisted users pay 0%
  • Referrers earn 20% of fees from their referrals
  • Fees support bot development and operations
