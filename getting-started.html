Getting Started

Follow this step-by-step guide to set up GHOSTwire and execute your first trade. Each step shows exactly what you'll see in the bot.

What You Need

• A Telegram account
• 0.01+ SOL in your wallet
• A Telegram channel that posts token addresses

Step 1: Start GHOSTwire

Open @ghowr_bot on Telegram and send /start.

What happens:

  • Bot creates your W1 wallet automatically
  • Wallet is derived from your Telegram ID
  • No private keys stored on any server
  • You get a clean welcome message with all options
